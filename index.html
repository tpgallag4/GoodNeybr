What We Do

GoodNeybr is a platform that connects Boston homeowners and HOAs with trusted local home service providers. Our goal is to make property management simpler, more transparent, and more cost-effective.

Whether you're managing an entire building or just need help with a one-off project in your unit, GoodNeybr gives you the tools to take control. For HOAs, we offer a centralized hub to manage vendors, track payments, organize condo documents, and oversee treasury services — all in one place.

Need to paint a bedroom, fix a leak, or replace an appliance in your unit? GoodNeybr is your one-stop shop. We collect quotes from qualified vendors in your area, compare options based on price and quality, and help you make informed decisions. Once you've selected a vendor, we'll manage scheduling and payments to keep things seamless and stress-free.

Who We Are

Hi — we're Mara and Tim (and Goose!), the founders of GoodNeybr. We live in Boston and created this platform after running into the same frustrations many condo owners face. We were paying hundreds of dollars in monthly HOA fees without feeling like we were getting our money's worth.

We built GoodNeybr to give our neighbors a better alternative — one that's more transparent, competitive, and local. Along the way, we discovered a lot of fantastic service providers in the Boston area — but finding them took way too much digging. We want to make it easier for others to find, hire, and support the best vendors in the neighborhood.
